Blink vs LookAway: Full Comparison (2026)

Updated April 2026

Both Blink and LookAway help you take healthy screen breaks, but they take very different approaches. Here's how they compare.

Platform Support

Blink runs on macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android from day one. LookAway is macOS-only with an iOS companion app (LookAway Mirror). If you use Windows or Linux, LookAway isn't an option.

Pricing

Blink is free for core features. Pro is $9.99/year. LookAway starts at $19 one-time purchase with no free tier. For teams, Blink is $4.99/seat/month vs LookAway's $29/seat (no team dashboard).

Features LookAway Doesn't Have

  • Pomodoro timer
  • Custom reminders (drink water, stretch, etc.)
  • Scheduled breaks (fixed-time lunch breaks)
  • Wind-down mode
  • Android companion app
  • Team dashboard with admin controls
  • Settings sync across devices
  • Automations & webhooks
  • JSON export/import

What LookAway Does Well

LookAway is a polished, native macOS app with Liquid Glass design for macOS Tahoe, deep AppleScript/Shortcuts integration, and tight macOS Focus Filters support. If you're exclusively on Mac and want the most native experience, LookAway is well-made.

The Verdict

If you need cross-platform support, a free tier, Pomodoro, or team features — Blink is the clear choice. If you're Mac-only and prefer a native Swift app, LookAway is worth considering.
