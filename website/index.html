Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 20-20-20 rule?

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This reduces eye strain from prolonged screen use. Blink automates this with smart break reminders.

Is Blink really free?

Yes! Core features — break reminders, blink/posture nudges, basic stats, and all 5 platforms — are completely free. Pro adds advanced features like Smart Pause, Pomodoro, and custom reminders for $9.99/year.

Does Blink work on all platforms?

Blink runs natively on macOS, Windows, and Linux desktops, plus iOS and Android mobile. One codebase, same features everywhere.

How does Smart Pause work?

Blink detects when you're in a meeting (camera/mic active), watching video, in a fullscreen app, or using a focus app — and automatically pauses break reminders until you're done.

Can I use Blink with my team?

Yes! The Team plan ($4.99/seat/month) includes an admin dashboard, centralized settings, aggregate health scores, and license management.

How is Blink different from other break reminder apps?

Blink is free, cross-platform (Mac + Windows + Linux + mobile), and includes Pomodoro, custom reminders, smart pause, and team features. Most alternatives are single-platform or lack these features.
